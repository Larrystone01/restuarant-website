Our Story

Built on Fire, Flavour & Family

Tass Place started as a backyard restaurant in Ilorin. Twelve years later, we still cook with the same wood fire and the same obsession with flavour.

🔥

Real Hardwood, Every Day

No gas shortcuts. Every protein grills over seasoned hardwood and African oak for that irreplaceable smoky depth.

🌿

Sourced Locally, Weekly

We buy direct from markets and farms across Lagos and Ogun State. Fresh ingredients arrive three times a week — no freezing.

👨‍🍳

Cooks Who Care

Our team of 8 cooks has an average of 9 years in the kitchen. They know Nigerian flavour from memory, not from a recipe card.

🏡

A Space That Feels Like Home

Private dining alcoves, open-air courtyard seating and a warm, unhurried atmosphere — because the best meals take time.
